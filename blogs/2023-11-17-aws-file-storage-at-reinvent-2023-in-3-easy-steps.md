<h4><strong>STG316 | Increase your database agility with Amazon FSx</strong></h4> 
<p>Migrating databases like SQL Server, PostgreSQL, Oracle, and SAP HANA to the cloud is a critical part of your journey. In this workshop, explore self-managed databases on Amazon EC2 with Amazon FSx, and get hands-on with SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and MySQL. Discover how the advanced snapshot, cloning, backup, and replication features of Amazon FSx can reduce your RPO/RTO from hours to seconds. Then, going beyond operations, see a demonstration of how to accelerate development and refresh cycles, using zero-space cloning to instantly provide your developers with their own copies of the database. You must bring your laptop to participate. <a href="https://hub.reinvent.awsevents.com/attendee-portal/catalog/?search=stg316" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Reserve your seat now.</a></p> 
<h4><strong>STG403 | Get insights faster: Accelerate your Amazon S3 data lake</strong></h4> 
<p>Get insights faster by accelerating Amazon S3 datasets with Amazon FSx for Lustre. FSx for Lustre is deeply integrated with Amazon S3, enabling you to access and process S3 data from a high-performance file system. In this workshop, learn how to process large S3 workloads with submillisecond latency. Create an FSx for Lustre file system, link it to your S3 bucket, spin up an Amazon EC2 cluster, and mount the FSx for Lustre file system. Learn how Lustre presents S3 objects as files to Amazon EC2 instances, providing fast throughput and high IOPS that scale out with large file systems. You must bring your laptop to participate. <a href="https://hub.reinvent.awsevents.com/attendee-portal/catalog/?search=stg403" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Reserve your seat now.</a></p>
